Owlintel helps you to
organize and make more
sense out of your life...
Owlintel is designed to help make it easier for you to do things like organize your thinking, feelings, emotions, meanings,and values.

Owlintel makes it easier for
you to ask the right questions...
Human thinking involves asking questions and getting answers. Owlintel makes it easier for you to ask the right questions, and consequently get the right answers, by guiding you along the process. Owlintel helps you to understand the detailed processs of inquiring, struggling to understand, and affirming the truth of what you have understood.

Discovering truth is a
personal experience...
Owlintel makes it easier for you to discover the meaning and values that define you as a human being. This is done through a simple user interface in which you enter text into a text field, choose from drop down menus, and read simple text messages based on philosophical cognition theory, as you go about asking questions and seeking out answers.
